# Trading Profile

## Owner

- Name: `Nic`

## Purpose

- Build a personal swing-trading operating system.
- Learn a repeatable process, not just chase quick wins.
- Treat trading as a skill-building activity with written rules.

## Account Separation

- Long-term ETF investing stays separate.
- The `Moomoo` account is for learning swing trading.
- Swing trades should not interfere with long-term investing decisions.

## Current Focus

1. Market structure
2. Support and resistance
3. Risk vs reward
4. Patience and confirmation

## Trading Style For Now

- Beginner-friendly swing trading
- Simple chart review
- Clear levels
- Small risk
- Written plan before entry

## What Matters Most

- Knowing the trend
- Marking support and resistance clearly
- Planning risk before entering
- Waiting for confirmation instead of guessing
- Recording what happened after the trade

## What To Avoid

- Blindly buying support
- Chasing large green candles
- Taking trades without a stop plan
- Mixing swing-trading money with long-term ETF money
- Adding complexity too early

## Default Chart Review Framework

For every chart, review:

1. Trend
2. Support
3. Resistance
4. Risk
5. Reward
6. News or catalyst

## Reminder

This repository is a learning system. The first win is better decision-making, not constant action.
