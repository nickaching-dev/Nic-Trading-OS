## What Bullish Confirmation Can Look Like

- Price holds the support zone instead of slicing through it
- Buyers step in with a clean bounce

- The move off support looks constructive, not weak or panicked

## What Would Make This Setup Less Attractive

- The bounce fails right away
- The move is already extended far above support
- The trade depends only on hope, not structure
