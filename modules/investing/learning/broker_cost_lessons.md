## Durable Lessons

- `IBKR` charges depend on the pricing plan, exchange, currency, order type, and whether the order is whole-share or fractional.
- Buy and sell commissions can each have separate minimum charges.
- Small London-listed fractional orders can be hit hard by minimum commissions.
- Currency conversion is a separate potential cost.
- GST, exchange, clearing, and regulatory charges may be added on top of commission.
- The Activity Statement is the definitive record for what was actually charged.
- `Moomoo` margin interest only applies when funds are actually borrowed, often because of a negative currency cash balance.

## Working Rule

- Check likely commissions, conversion costs, and margin exposure before placing small or foreign-market orders.
