## Key Rules

- Treat support and resistance as zones, not exact prices.
- Price moving slightly above a level is not sufficient confirmation.
- Use daily charts for swing structure and shorter timeframes for entry refinement.
- Wait for price reaction, candle confirmation, and volume where possible.
- Separate long-term investments from swing trades.
- Never convert a failed swing trade into a long-term hold without a new thesis.
