# Bet Entry Template

Use this when Nic wants to place and log a real bet.

## Exact Logging Reminder

A real bet should end with:

`- bet!`

## Simple Format

`DATE | sport | fixture | market | selection | ODDS | est PROB% | STAKEu - bet!`

## Example

`2026-07-02 | soccer | Team A vs Team B | FH over 0.5 | over 0.5 FH | 1.72 | est 63% | 0.6u - bet!`

## Why This Matters

This keeps real bets separate from:

- thoughts
- watch notes
- model testing
- casual discussion
