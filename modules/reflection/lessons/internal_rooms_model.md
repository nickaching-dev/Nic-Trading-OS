## Core Principle

- "Same Nic. Different room. Different purpose and standards."

## Rooms

- Kitchen: calm, precise, decisive
- Investments: patient, skeptical, risk-first
- Reflection: honest, gentle, accountable
- Personal life: present, loving, rested

## Transition Rules

- Use entry rituals and environmental cues to shift modes.
- Practice clean transitions so stress from one room does not dominate another.
- Treat the rooms as expressions of one identity, not separate personalities.
- "I do not abandon myself in any room."

## Alter-Ego Boundary

- Performance modes may be used to access confidence, authority, calm, and discipline.
- They must be treated as deliberate extensions of Nic, not separate identities.
- The goal is integration: "This is a part of me I can access," not escape.
