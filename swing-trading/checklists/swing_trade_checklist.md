# Swing Trade Checklist

Use this before every trade. If several answers are `no`, pass on the trade and wait for a cleaner setup.

## Chart Check

- [ ] I can clearly describe the trend.
- [ ] I have marked support.
- [ ] I have marked resistance or target zones.
- [ ] The chart looks clear enough that I do not need to force the idea.

## Setup Check

- [ ] I know why this stock is on my watchlist.
- [ ] There is a simple catalyst or reason for attention.
- [ ] I am not buying blindly into support.
- [ ] I am waiting for confirmation.
- [ ] I am not chasing after a large move.

## Risk Check

- [ ] I know my planned entry.
- [ ] I know my stop.
- [ ] I know my first target.
- [ ] The planned reward is at least `2x` the planned risk.
- [ ] The dollar risk fits my account rules.

## Behavior Check

- [ ] This trade matches my written rules.
- [ ] I feel calm, not rushed or emotional.
- [ ] I am not trying to win back a recent loss.
- [ ] I have written the setup in my journal before entering.

## Final Decision

- [ ] If this checklist is mostly `yes`, I can consider the trade.
- [ ] If I am rationalizing the setup, I will skip it.
