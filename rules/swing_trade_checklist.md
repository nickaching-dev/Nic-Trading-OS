# Swing Trade Checklist

Use this before every trade. If several boxes stay unchecked, the default decision is `skip`.

## Chart Read

- [ ] I can explain the trend in plain English.
- [ ] I have marked support.
- [ ] I have marked resistance or target zones.
- [ ] The chart looks clear enough that I am not forcing an idea.

## Setup Quality

- [ ] I know why this stock is on my watchlist.
- [ ] I understand the basic catalyst or reason for attention.
- [ ] I am not buying support blindly.
- [ ] I am waiting for confirmation.
- [ ] I am not chasing a large move after it already ran.

## Risk Plan

- [ ] I know my planned entry.
- [ ] I know where the trade is wrong.
- [ ] I know my stop.
- [ ] I know my first target.
- [ ] The planned reward is at least `2x` the planned risk.
- [ ] The dollar risk fits my account rules.

## Behavior Check

- [ ] I feel calm, not rushed.
- [ ] I am not trading out of boredom.
- [ ] I am not revenge trading after a loss.
- [ ] I have written the setup down before entering.

## Final Decision

- [ ] If the setup is clear and the risk is defined, I can consider the trade.
- [ ] If I am rationalizing the trade, I will pass.
